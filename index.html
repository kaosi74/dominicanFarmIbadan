In recent years, organic foods have gained popularity among health-conscious consumers, driven by their perceived health benefits and environmental advantages. Organic foods are produced without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, genetically modified organisms (GMOs), or artificial additives. This farming approach often results in produce that retains higher levels of nutrients, antioxidants, and phytochemicals, which are beneficial for overall health.
